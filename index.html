About us

Mavin's taste for delivering the utmost value, has inspired the company's expansion into innovative services such as partnerships, content production, publishing and marketing. It currently houses 11 artistes and has overseen a label record release of 16 albums so far.

We are committed to adding new voices and faces to the label. We encourage prospective artistes to keep pushing out their sample covers on all social media platforms till they get their big break.

The label won the award for Best Record Label of the Year at the 2014 City People Entertainment Awards.
